Odela Railway Station (2022) – Movie Review, Cast, Story, and More

Odela Railway Station is a 2022 Telugu-language crime thriller that blends suspense, drama, and rural atmosphere to deliver a gripping experience. Produced by Sampath Nandi and directed by Ashok Teja, this film is inspired by true incidents from the village of Odela in Telangana.

Plot Summary

Set in the village of Odela, the story follows a series of mysterious murders of newlywed women. The village lives in fear as the killer remains unknown. A young trainee IPS officer (played by Sai Ronak) takes charge of the case and goes undercover to uncover the truth. The suspense builds as secrets unravel near the Odela railway station — a place that holds the key to the crimes.

What Makes It Special

  • Based on True Events: The film draws from real incidents in rural Telangana.
  • Rural Thriller Vibe: The setting, dialect, and atmosphere give it a raw and authentic feel.
  • Strong Performances: Hebah Patel and Vashista Simha deliver emotionally charged performances.
  • Music & Background Score: The haunting score enhances the suspense.
