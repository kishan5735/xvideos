Alappuzha Gymkhana (2025)

A Malayalam Sports Comedy-Drama by Khalid Rahman

Plot Summary

A group of young friends from Alappuzha, who fail their Plus Two exams, decide to pursue college admissions through the sports quota—despite no experience in sports. They choose boxing and embark on a journey filled with trials, friendship, and self-discovery.

Cast & Crew

  • Director: Khalid Rahman
  • Writers: Khalid Rahman, Sreeni Saseendran
  • Dialogues: Ratheesh Ravi
  • Music: Vishnu Vijay
  • Lead Cast: Naslen K. Gafoor, Lukman Avaran, Ganapathi, Sandeep Pradeep, Franco Francis, Anagha Ravi, Shine Tom Chacko

Box Office

The movie grossed over ₹56 crore worldwide within 11 days and became one of the top Malayalam hits of 2025.

OTT Release

Expected on or around June 4, 2025 on platforms like Amazon Prime Video or Disney+ Hotstar.
